What is Join?

JOIN is a visual work management tool that enables large and small teams to manage, plan and prioritize their work together.
This creates an efficient and productive organizational environment that facilitates collaborative work.
With JOIN, existing work steps can be recorded or new tasks can be added in a very simple and user-friendly way.
The simplicity and intuitive way create a unique work experience and increase the friends and fun of collaborative work.
In addition, the management of individual tasks can also be mapped for a user.
Before you start, check out our short overview of the most important must-know features of JOIN.

How to use it

1.

Summary: In the summary view you can see at a single glance which urgent tasks are pending and which are already done. So you can always get the overview and start the day.

2.

Board: Here you will find all your tasks listed in the specific process level or category. You can easily move the tasks by drag&drop. It is also possible to assign the task to a contact and to prioritize the task.

3.

Add Task: Create a task with title & description and assign it to one or more users. You also have to select the category, due date and the priority of the task. Subtasks can be created if you like to.

4.

Contacts: Here you can create new contacts and get an overview of existing contacts. Also editing or deleting can be done here.
